About the Chapter

Where Geeks Connect, Code, and Conquer.

The GeeksforGeeks Student Chapter at our university is a vibrant community of passionate coders and technology enthusiasts. We aim to bridge the gap between academic theory and industry requirements by providing a platform for students to practice coding, solve complex problems, and prepare for top-tier technical interviews.

Our chapter hosts workshops, hackathons, and guest lectures by industry leaders from companies like Google, Meta, and Microsoft — all to help you grow beyond the classroom.
